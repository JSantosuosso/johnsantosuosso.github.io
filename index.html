Engineering Portfolio

Selected Projects

Mandalorian Wrist‑Mounted Flame Thrower

A wearable wrist-mounted flamethrower system inspired by Mandalorian armor. Integrated fuel delivery, ignition control, and safety mechanisms built into a forearm-mounted housing. CAD‑designed, fabricated, and tested for controlled flame projection.

Cephalobot – Soft Robotic Squid

A soft‑robotic biomimetic squid using silicone molding, distributed pneumatic actuation, and flexible linkages. Designed to replicate cephalopod‑like undulating motion for research in soft robotics and underwater locomotion.

VT CRO Trimodal Robot

A multifunctional robot for the Center for Research in Ocean Engineering at Virginia Tech. Capable of walking, rolling, and swimming through a trimodal actuation architecture. Contributed design, modeling, and mechanical integration.

Follow‑Me Drone

An autonomous quadcopter using computer vision and GPS‑assisted tracking to follow a target user. Features onboard ESP32‑based communication, PID control loops, and custom‑designed tracking algorithms.

e‑Nable @ Virginia Tech – Low‑Cost Prosthetic Hand

A 3D‑printed mechanical prosthetic hand for underserved communities. Designed for low‑cost production and modular assembly with improved grip performance and adjustable tendon routing.

CanSat @ VT 2024 – Atmospheric Probe

Designed and built a deployable probe for CanSat 2024. Features telemetry, microcontroller‑based sensor suite, aerodynamic stability system, and controlled descent mechanisms.

Cardboard Bridge

A structural engineering challenge project: designed, analyzed, and built a fully cardboard bridge capable of supporting significant loads through optimized geometry and load distribution.
